Plaid API Functions

OBJECTIVES

Pull and Return/Store User’s Banking Data via Plaid API Requests

The main goals were focusing on right now is creating scripts with functions that takes care of, but not limited to, the following tasks:

  1. Connects to Plaid’s authentication system, Link, and retrieves the user’s account credentials and verify the account holder’s identity to match the user information we collected for the user.
  2. Collect the user’s current account balances and also for each of the past 24 months, and store this data.
  3. Collect user’s Last-Year Income, Projected Yearly Income for current year, Income Streams, and # of Income Streams.
  4. Collect Spending Transactions and break down vs. income, by category.
  5. Verify employer information.
